Radiation

An end-of-year note

As 2025 slowly finds its ending, I wanted to leave behind a few honest words — just for you.

Somewhere between ordinary days and quiet moments, you became a gentle constant. Not loud. Not forced. Just… present in a way that mattered.

Some people brighten a room. Some brighten a year. And some do it without ever realizing they are doing it at all.

I don’t know what the next chapter looks like, but before this one closes, I wanted you to know this — I’m genuinely grateful for you.

— From me, at the end of 2025
